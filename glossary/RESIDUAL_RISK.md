# Residual Risk

## Definition

**Residual risk** is the **remaining exposure** after remediation, compensating controls, or **explicit risk acceptance**. It answers: *What risk is still present even after we acted?*

Residual risk is **not zero** when a ticket closes—it may be accepted, transferred, mitigated, or unknown until re-validated.

## SentriScope Usage

Operational programs track residual risk for reprioritization and acceptance review. Reference: `KID-OIN-0001`. Term authority only—no scoring mechanics here.
