# Hypothesis

## Definition

A **hypothesis** in security investigation is an **explicit, testable statement** of what may be true about a security question. A complete hypothesis identifies confirming evidence, disconfirming evidence, and scope boundaries.

Hypotheses are models under test—not conclusions. Multiple competing hypotheses reduce confirmation bias.
