# Operational Signal

## Definition

An **operational signal** is a **normalized internal observation** from security operations—alerts, findings, state changes, or metrics—that **feeds** operational intelligence.

Signals are **inputs**. [Operational intelligence](./OPERATIONAL_INTELLIGENCE.md) (`KID-GLS-0006`) is the **decision-support layer** that ranks and explains where attention should go.
