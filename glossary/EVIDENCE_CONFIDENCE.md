# Evidence Confidence

## Definition

**Evidence confidence** is the **justified strength of belief** that a specific security conclusion is supported by available evidence. It reflects corroboration, validation, specificity, and contradiction handling—not tool severity labels or alert counts alone.

Confidence may be expressed in defined tiers (e.g., high, medium, low) with team-calibrated criteria.

## SentriScope Usage

Investigation and intelligence workflows may surface confidence indicators. Threat intelligence confidence is governed in `KID-TIN-0001`. This glossary entry is the cross-domain term authority.
