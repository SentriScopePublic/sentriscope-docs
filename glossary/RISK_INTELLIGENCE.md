# Risk Intelligence

## Definition

**Risk intelligence** is the practice of **prioritizing security findings and decisions** using **context**, **validated evidence**, and **threat inputs**—so analysts and stakeholders know what matters first and why.

Risk intelligence supports **operational security decisions**. It is distinct from:

- **Enterprise risk management (ERM)** — organizational governance frameworks  
- **Compliance programs** — control attestation and audit cycles  
- **Generic risk management consulting content** — policy and ISO frameworks  

SentriScope's public education in this domain focuses on **how analysts make better prioritization decisions**.

## SentriScope Usage

SentriScope derives explainable risk outcomes from canonical context and intelligence inputs. References: `KID-ARC-0001`, `KID-OIN-0001`, `KID-PLT-0004`. This entry does not describe implementation.
