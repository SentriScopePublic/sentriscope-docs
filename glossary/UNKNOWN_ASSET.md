# Unknown Asset

## Definition

An **unknown asset** is an asset that **exists in the environment** but **lacks sufficient context** for confident security decisions.

The uncertainty is about **significance and relationships**—ownership, business role, exposure, dependencies—not merely the absence of a CMDB record. Discovery may surface the entity; decision support still requires context enrichment.
