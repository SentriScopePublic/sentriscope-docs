# What is the difference between threat and operational intelligence?

## Short answer

**Threat intelligence** = **external** environment (actors, campaigns, exploitation). **Operational intelligence** = **internal** state (assets, telemetry, controls, queues). Both inform decisions; SER-007 integrates them.
