# What is security intelligence as decision support?

## Short answer

**Security intelligence as decision support** means integrating **threat intelligence**, **operational intelligence**, and **Volume 1 foundations** (evidence, investigation, risk, attack paths) to produce **explainable, auditable** prioritization and investigation actions—humans remain accountable for decisions.

## Explanation

Volume 2 closes with this thesis: intelligence **informs** judgment; it does not replace evidence validation, risk ownership, or investigation discipline.
