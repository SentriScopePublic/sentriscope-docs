# What is the difference between investigation and incident response?

## Short answer

**Investigation** builds **validated understanding** with evidence. **Incident response** executes **containment and recovery** under time pressure. Both may run during an active incident.

## Explanation

| Investigation | Incident Response |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Evidence-backed conclusions | Stabilized systems |
| May span weeks | Minutes to hours critical |
| Scope and root cause | Reduced blast radius |
