# Can security intelligence override evidence?

## Short answer

**No.** **Validated evidence** leads conclusions about what occurred. When intelligence conflicts with evidence, **investigate the conflict**—revise intelligence mapping, confidence, or scope. Do not declare outcomes on intelligence alone.

## Explanation

Intelligence suggests **where to look** and **what urgency is plausible**. Evidence determines **what happened**. Conflicts are normal and signal mapping errors, stale reporting, or incomplete collection.
