# Does operational intelligence replace risk analysis?

## Short answer

**No.** OI supplies **live operational state** to inform risk rank. [Risk prioritization](../knowledge/RISK_PRIORITIZATION_IN_PRACTICE.md) and acceptance decisions still require analyst judgment.
