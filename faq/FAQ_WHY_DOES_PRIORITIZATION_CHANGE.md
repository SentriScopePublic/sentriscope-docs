# Why does prioritization change?

## Short answer

Operational prioritization **changes** when context, intelligence, exposure, relationships, or team capacity shifts. Rank is a **living judgment**, not a fixed severity sort frozen at creation time.

## Explanation

The same finding can move up or down a queue when threat intelligence activates, exposure context clarifies, correlation links new entities, or business priority shifts. Stable rank without stated rationale usually means stale decision support—not mature operations.
