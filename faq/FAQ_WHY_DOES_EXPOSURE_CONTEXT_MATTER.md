# Why does exposure context matter?

## Short answer

**Exposure context** matters because the **same weakness** can warrant **different urgency** depending on asset placement, reachability, business function, and environment—not severity alone.

## Explanation

Context layers—where the exposure lives, who can reach it, what it connects to, and what operational or intelligence signals apply—transform a catalog finding into a **decision**. Without context, teams over-remediate low-impact instances and under-respond to reachable paths toward critical assets. Context is what makes exposure **explainable** to stakeholders and **actionable** for analysts.
