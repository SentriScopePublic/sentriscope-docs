# What is security intelligence?

## Short answer

**Security intelligence** is the **integrated use** of **threat intelligence** (external) and **operational intelligence** (internal)—alongside **evidence** and **risk context**—to support **explainable security decisions**. It is a decision discipline, not a third feed type.

## Explanation

Organizations already consume TI and OI separately. Security intelligence is how those inputs **reconcile** into one auditable priority narrative—without overriding validated evidence.
