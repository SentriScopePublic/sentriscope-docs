# What makes applications decision support?

## Short answer

Applications become **decision support** when **context**—business function, relationships, data class, and provenance—helps explain **why** findings rank differently and **where** attention should go, not when they exist only as service catalog records.

## Explanation

A service inventory alone answers *what endpoints exist*. Decision support answers *what business capability matters now* and *why*. That requires enriched canonical context tied to findings, access paths, attack paths, and investigations—so analysts and stakeholders can act with explainable priority rather than flat severity lists.
