# How do investigations support operations?

## Short answer

**Investigations** resolve uncertainty that daily operations cannot—testing hypotheses with evidence—then **return outcomes** that refine intelligence use, exposure scope, and future queue rank. Operations and investigation are complementary, not interchangeable.

## Explanation

Operations applies Security Intelligence to recurring judgments under time pressure. Investigation asks *what happened* and *what is proven* when signals conflict or stakes require evidence depth.

[Security Operations vs Incident Response](../knowledge/SECURITY_OPERATIONS_VS_INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md) (`KID-CON-0132`) distinguishes continuous operational judgment from incident-centric response, building on [Security Investigation vs Incident Response](../knowledge/SECURITY_INVESTIGATION_VS_INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md) (`KID-CON-0005`).
