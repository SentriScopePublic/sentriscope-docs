# What is operational feedback?

## Short answer

**Operational feedback** is **structured learning from daily security decisions**—what happened after a prioritization, deferral, or coordination choice—and how that outcome should change the next operational cycle.

## Explanation

Feedback is not a metric dashboard alone or an incident post-mortem reserved for breaches. It closes the loop between judgment and outcome: Was the deferral justified? Did intelligence that drove rank remain valid? Teams that capture operational feedback improve [operational decision cycles](../knowledge/OPERATIONAL_DECISION_CYCLES.md) without conflating feedback with compliance checkboxing.
