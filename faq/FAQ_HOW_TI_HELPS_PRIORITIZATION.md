# How does threat intelligence help prioritization?

## Short answer

TI surfaces **active exploitation**, **campaign relevance**, and **technique alignment** with your exposed assets—context that CVSS and static scans cannot provide alone.

## Explanation

TI **informs** rank; it does not replace risk judgment. Document why intelligence changed priority.
