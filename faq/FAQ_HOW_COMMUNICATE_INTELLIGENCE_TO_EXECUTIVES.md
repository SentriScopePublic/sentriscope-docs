# How should analysts communicate intelligence to executives?

## Short answer

Lead with: **(1)** decision or recommendation, **(2)** why now (integrated TI + OI summary), **(3)** confidence level, **(4)** evidence status, **(5)** residual risk if no action, **(6)** next update cadence. Avoid raw feeds, IoC dumps, and unqualified "critical threat" language.

## Explanation

Executives need **decisions with rationale**, not intelligence volume. Integrated security intelligence should arrive as one coherent narrative—not separate TI and OI briefings that may contradict.
