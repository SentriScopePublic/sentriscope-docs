# What is threat intelligence?

## Short answer

**Threat intelligence** is validated **external information** about threats—actors, campaigns, techniques, and exploitation context. It helps teams **prioritize and investigate** when mapped to your assets and evidence.

## Explanation

Intelligence becomes actionable when it **changes a decision**—patch urgency, hunt scope, or investigation depth—not when it fills a dashboard.
