# Why does correlation matter for security decisions?

## Short answer

Correlation matters for decisions when linked observations reveal **compound significance** that no single record shows—changing rank, investigation scope, exposure reduction order, and executive narrative. Without it, teams optimize fragments while missing shared root causes and structural choke points.

## Explanation

Deduplication reduces noise; **decision-quality correlation** changes what teams prioritize and how they scope investigation. Ask: *Would any single observation alone justify this action?* If linkage changes rank or scope, correlation is decision support.
