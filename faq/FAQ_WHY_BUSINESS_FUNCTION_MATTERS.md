# Why does business function matter in security?

## Short answer

**Business function**—the **organizational purpose** an application serves—determines **escalation paths**, **stakeholder communication**, and **why identical technical findings** demand different security responses. Technical severity alone cannot answer what revenue, regulatory, or customer capability is at stake.

## Explanation

Two applications with the same vulnerability score can warrant opposite actions when one supports internal training and the other processes customer payments. Business function connects technical events to **why the organization cares**—the same analytical lens Volume 3 applies to assets and identities, applied to software capabilities.
