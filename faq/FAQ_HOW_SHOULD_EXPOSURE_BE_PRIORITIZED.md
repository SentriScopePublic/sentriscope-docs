# How should exposure be prioritized?

## Short answer

Prioritize exposure using **context**—reachability, asset and application significance, [attack exposure](../glossary/ATTACK_EXPOSURE.md), paths, and intelligence—not **severity scores alone**.

## Explanation

Flat vulnerability rankings treat every instance equally. Exposure prioritization asks **what the weakness means here**: Can it be reached? Does it sit on a path to critical assets? Does threat or operational intelligence change urgency? Risk intelligence and exposure context together produce **explainable** order of work—what to reduce first and why stakeholders should agree.
