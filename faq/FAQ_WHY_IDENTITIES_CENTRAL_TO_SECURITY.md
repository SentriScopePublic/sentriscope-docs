# Why are identities central to modern security?

## Short answer

**Identities are central** because most attacks **pivot through credentials and access**. Identity context determines **blast radius**, **investigation scope**, and **explainable prioritization** across assets and applications—not merely who can log in.

## Explanation

Modern environments distribute access across cloud roles, service principals, federated users, and machine identities. Security outcomes depend on whether teams understand **who can reach what**, **how privilege amplifies impact**, and **how identity context changes decisions**—the same analytical lens Volume 3 applies to assets, applied to the actors that move through them.
