# How do analysts explain risk to executives?

## Short answer

Lead with **business impact** and **recommended action** on a **small prioritized set**. Support with context and confidence. Avoid raw Critical counts and CVE lists.

Framework: [Risk Communication](../knowledge/RISK_COMMUNICATION.md) (`KID-CON-0033`).
