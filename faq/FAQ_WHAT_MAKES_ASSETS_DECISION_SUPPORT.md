# What makes assets decision support?

## Short answer

Assets become **decision support** when **context**—criticality, relationships, environment, and provenance—helps explain **why** findings rank differently and **where** attention should go, not when they exist only as inventory records.

## Explanation

An asset inventory alone answers *what exists*. Decision support answers *what matters now* and *why*. That requires enriched canonical context tied to findings, exposures, attack paths, and investigations—so analysts and stakeholders can act with explainable priority rather than flat severity lists.
