# What is an investigation hypothesis?

## Short answer

An **investigation hypothesis** is a **testable claim** about what may be happening, plus what evidence would **confirm or refute** it.

## Explanation

Term authority: [Hypothesis](../glossary/HYPOTHESIS.md) (`KID-GLS-0018`).

Guide: [Investigation Hypothesis](../knowledge/INVESTIGATION_HYPOTHESIS.md) (`KID-CON-0020`).

## Common mistake

Treating the first alert narrative as the hypothesis without listing disconfirming searches.
